About Barkio

Barkio is a new dog monitoring app coming to your Android, iOS, macOS, Windows and Linux. Barkio will help you to stay in touch with your dog when it's home alone. Make sure your dog is safe, see what it's doing and never miss another cute moment.

  • Watch live video of your dog
  • Talk to your dog remotely
  • Check overview of all activities
  • No hardware cameras needed
